Challenges !

Many Retail Businesses in the UK employ various methods to underreport or manipulate sales Data.
These Methods include the use of electronics sales suppression(ESS) software, utilizing secondary electronic point of sale (EPOS) systems, avoiding the use of EPOS systems altogether , and voiding sales, among other tactics.
This Practice is prevalent among retail businesses serving both business to consumer (B2C) and business to business (B2B) customers.

By Reporting lower or no Profits, these businesses effectively reduce or eliminate their tax liabilities.
